Revenue Model

Multiple streams.
Zero from users.

Every pre-approved, UAE Pass verified buyer referred to FAB, ADCB, ADIB or Emirates NBD generates a referral fee. Eqary delivers what banks have never had — fully qualified, KYC-complete buyers at the point of intent.
Activates from launch

Highest LTV · Phase 5
Rent to Own
The highest-lifetime-value product in the stack. Uniquely, Eqary earns whether a contract completes or unwinds. A tenant who buys at term arrives at closing already verified, bank-approved, and committed — acquired at a fraction of the cost of a cold transaction. A tenant who exits early triggers a break fee. Both outcomes generate revenue.
Revenue on completion and on exit
Four revenue events
Origination fee
Charged when the RTO contract is signed by both parties
Equity management fee
Monthly, for maintaining the equity ledger and DARI registration throughout the term
Conversion fee
When the purchase option is exercised and the DARI title transfer completes
Break fee
If either party exits before the term ends, Eqary takes a commission from the settlement. The contract unwinding is itself a revenue event.

Phase 5 · Final Milestone · Q3 2027

Rent to Own —
the ownership bridge.

71% of Abu Dhabi's residents rent. Most want to own. Eqary gives them a path — built on the tenant credit data, bank relationships, and platform trust accumulated across Phases 1 to 4.

The gap it closes

Traditional homeownership requires a 20–25% down payment that takes years to accumulate, and a mortgage tied to employer-verified income. Rent to Own removes both barriers. Monthly payments build equity. That equity becomes the down payment. The bank assesses creditworthiness on demonstrated payment behaviour — not employment status.

⏱
Why it comes last

RTO requires three things to exist first: a tenant database with verified payment history (Phase 3), bank partnerships capable of structuring a bespoke financing product (Phase 2), and platform trust earned through months of clean transactions. None of those can be shortcut. Q3 2027 is the targeted entry point for full ownership capabilities.
